Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between tournaments?

The tournaments offered differ in a variety of ways. This may include the time control used, the number of rounds, the entry fee and the prize money.

What are the different time controls?

Our tournaments are generally split into 3 time control categories:

  • Bullet: 1-2 minutes per player per round
  • Blitz: 3-14 minutes per player per round
  • Standard:15+ minutes per player per round
Why can't I buy multiple tickets for the same event?

All our tournaments require each player to have their own account registered on our website.
